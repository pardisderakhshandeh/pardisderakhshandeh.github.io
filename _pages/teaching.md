<p class="teaching-intro">
  My teaching spans syntax, sociolinguistics, and language documentation, ranging from large undergraduate
  lectures to graduate seminars and one-on-one fieldwork consultation. Alongside canonical courses like
  historical linguistics and the structure of English, I've designed and taught seminars built around my
  own research, including two on the grammar of understudied Iranian languages.
</p>
